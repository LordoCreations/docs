---
title: "Sensors"
description: "Various Sensors often used in FTC"
---

Other than motor encoders, sensors are connected to Control and Expansion Hubs through I2C or Digital 4-pin wires. Due to the wide variety of FTC-legal sensors, it is best to follow the official or relevant guides for whatever hardware you have.
